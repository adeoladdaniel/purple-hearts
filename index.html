About the Book

The book Purple Hearts was borne out of the love for the family unit and building the core values and character traits of Love, Compassion, Empathy, Integrity and emotionally stable individuals in the society For any Family unit to stand with the right foundation, mindset and values, it takes the quality of education the man and woman going into the Marriage Institution have and are willing to explore together as a couple.

  • Passing it on to their seeds(children) through observation, practice and then teaching.
  • Imagine each family unit in our Nation , community and circle of influence live out these core values....
  • What a loving, peaceful and united society we would have and enjoy.
  • Purple Hearts is storytelling. It's Engaging and Intriguing.
  • On every chapter you'll need to have a time of meditation whereby you Take Action.
